Delete Account

If you would like to delete your Family Wellness Diary account and associated data, please follow the process below.

Option 1: Delete from Inside the App

If account deletion is available in the app, please use the account or profile settings section to request deletion directly.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or need support, send an account deletion request to:

Please replace this with your actual support email before publishing, for example support@myfamilyhealthdiary.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your app account name, if applicable
  • A short note saying that you want your account deleted

What Will Be Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize personal account data and associated records linked to your account, subject to legal, compliance, fraud prevention, backup, and operational retention requirements.

Retention Exceptions

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for legal obligations, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or system backups that are overwritten on a normal schedule.

Processing Time

Please allow a reasonable period to verify and process deletion requests.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, please contact the same support email listed above.
